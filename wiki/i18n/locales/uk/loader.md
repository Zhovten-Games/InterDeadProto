# Ukrainian Loader Strings

Ukrainian variants of the loader module titles. Arrays under `boot.db`, `boot.camera`, `boot.lang`, `boot.templates`, and `boot.geo` feed the animated loader so each boot step can display rotating thematic phrases.[^1]
